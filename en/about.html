Our History

2025 - Present

At Integral Massage Therapy (TIM), we are exclusively dedicated to physical and emotional well-being through a wide variety of therapies. With over 8 years of experience, we have established ourselves as a reference center for professional massages in Recoleta.

We believe that taking care of the body is essential for a fulfilling life. That is why we have a team of highly trained therapists committed to offering a personalized and quality experience. For us, health is the foundation of daily well-being, and we reflect that in every session.

Thanks to the love and trust of our clients, we have experienced constant and sustained growth. Your support is what drives us to keep improving every day!

2019 - 2022

Despite the challenges brought by the pandemic, TIM stood strong thanks to the unconditional support of those who choose us year after year. These years strengthened us as a team and as a supportive space.

2017 - Opening of the center

We opened our doors in September 2017, at Ayacucho 1250, Recoleta. Since then, hundreds of people have trusted us to improve their quality of life through the power of therapeutic massage.
